About Me

Hello, my name is Trevor Hogland and I'm a rising Senior studying Information Analysis at the University of Michigan School of Information in Ann Arbor. I believe data is the future, and I'm passionate about uncovering and exploring trends across data. With experience in Python, SQL, Data Visualization, and Machine Learning, I'm equipped to apply my analytics skills to a variety of real-world problems. I'm a lifelong athlete and have grown my data skills through several sports analytics projects. These projects focused on free agency and player evaluation in the National Hockey League. When I'm not working hard at school, you're likely to see me on the golf course or playing hockey.
